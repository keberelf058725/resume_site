Custom Reports

Here are some of the reports we offer out of the box. All reports are designed to work with Kipu Health EMR but can be built to work with any EMR!

ALOS Analysis

Determine how long your patients are staying.

Incomplete Items

Generate a list of unsigned documents in your patient's charts.

Census Level Tracking

Track Census Trends over time and explore the underlying data.

Infection Control

Make short work of you infection control tracking by using your data to show infection sources.

Medication Re-orders

Generate a daily list of medications that contain stop dates for the next day.

Flag Tracking

Tracks flags that are entered into patient's charts

Employment and Legal Status Tracking

Use you documentation to your advantage and pull the information that you need to track.

Admissions Tracking

Generate a daily list of admissions from the previous day and their demographic information.

Discharge Tracking

Generate a daily list of discharges from the previous day and their demographic information.

Missing Lab Requisitions

Ensure that your patient's lab requisitions are being completed on time.



AMA Analysis

Dive into the data associated with AMAs to discover trends in the underlying data.



Custom End of the Year Report

We offer a deep dive into your data to analyze trends such as re-admissions and how they correlate to other data points like diagnoses.
